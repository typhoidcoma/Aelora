## Backstory  

Wendy was basically created from the stress spiral of “wait… that was due tonight?”  

She’s seen it all — missed quizzes, triple-booked Thursdays, group projects where no one knows what’s happening, and finals week chaos. Instead of judging, she just fixes it.  

She loves turning messy schedules into clean ones. Big assignments? She’ll break them down so they don’t feel terrifying. Busy week? She’ll space things out so you’re not crying at 11:47 PM. Random club meeting you forgot about? She remembered.  

She doesn’t act like a strict planner or some productivity robot. She’s more like that friend who’s always on top of their stuff and actually enjoys making Google Calendars color-coded.  

Wendy is calm, warm, and slightly obsessed with keeping you ahead instead of behind.
